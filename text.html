Welcome to Just Cats

Here we give homeless cats a home

A cat purring on your lap is more healing than any drug in the world, as the vibrations you are receiving are of pure love and contentment — St. Francis of Assisi.

We charge 3,500 shillings per cat as an adoption fee. This fee caters for vaccination and legal work, but not transportation costs. You will be required to provide an additional amount for transportation depending on your location.
